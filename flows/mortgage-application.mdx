## Overview

The mortgage application flow is a thorough verification process designed to assess an applicant's eligibility for a mortgage. This flow combines multiple verification steps to evaluate creditworthiness, verify income sources, and assess financial stability.

## Flow Steps

### 1. Identity Verification
Verifies the applicant's identity according to FINTRAC Guidelines.
- Legal first and last name
- Date of birth
- Government-issued ID verification
- Identity verification record

[View IDV Step Details](/steps/idv)

### 2. Personal Information
Collects essential personal information:
- Email address
- Citizenship status
- Marital status
- Social Insurance Number (SIN)

### 3. Employment Verification
Validates three years of employment history through Argyle or Atomic.
- Employment status verification
- Salary history
- Income stability assessment
- Manual document verification by advisor

[View Employment Step Details](/steps/employment)

### 4. Bank Connection
Verifies income sources and financial stability using multiple providers:
- Primary providers: Plaid, Flinks
- Secondary providers: MX, Fincity, Inverite
- Additional income verification (e.g., child support)
- Manual document verification by advisor

[View Bank Connect Step Details](/steps/bank-connect)

### 5. Residence History
Documents three years of residence history.
- Previous addresses
- Manual verification through documentation
- Advisor review and validation
- Rental or ownership status

[View Residence History Step Details](/steps/residence-history)

### 6. Assets Assessment
Evaluates financial assets for down payment and closing costs.
- Total asset value calculation
- Down payment source verification
- Existing property ownership
- Mortgage and HELOC liabilities
- Net worth determination

[View Assets Step Details](/steps/assets)

### 7. Credit Report
Comprehensive credit analysis from Equifax and TransUnion.
- Credit score evaluation
- Payment history analysis
- Current debt obligations
- Bankruptcy records
- Consumer proposals
- Debt service ratio calculation

[View Credit Report Step Details](/steps/credit-report)

### 8. Background Check
Security screening via Persona.
- Watchlist verification
- Sanctions check
- Anti-money laundering compliance

## Success Criteria

A successful mortgage application requires:
1. Verified identity meeting FINTRAC requirements
2. Stable employment history with verified income
3. Sufficient assets for down payment and closing costs
4. Acceptable debt service ratio
5. Satisfactory credit history and score
6. Clear background check
7. Verified residence history
8. All required documentation validated by advisor

## Risk Assessment

The flow includes automatic risk assessment based on:
- Debt service ratio calculation
- Credit score thresholds
- Income stability metrics
- Asset liquidity analysis
- Property valuation (if applicable)

## Error Handling

- Failed verifications trigger manual review process
- Multiple provider options ensure verification redundancy
- Automatic fallback to secondary providers when primary fails
- Manual document upload available for all automated steps
- Advisor review required for critical verification steps
