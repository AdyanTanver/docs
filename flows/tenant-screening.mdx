## Overview

The tenant screening flow is a comprehensive verification process that helps property managers and landlords make informed decisions about potential tenants. The flow combines multiple verification steps to create a complete profile of an applicant.

## Flow Steps

### 1. Identity Verification
Verifies the applicant's identity using government-issued documents.
- Legal first and last name
- Date of birth
- Identity verification record (FINTRAC Guide compliant)
- Selfie matching and liveness detection

[View IDV Step Details](/steps/idv)

### 2. Personal Information
Collects essential personal information:
- Email address
- Citizenship
- Marital status
- Social Insurance Number (SIN)

### 3. Employment Verification
Verifies three years of employment history using Argyle or Atomic.
- Employment status
- Salary data
- Manual document verification by advisor

[View Employment Step Details](/steps/employment)

### 4. Bank Connection
Verifies additional income sources using multiple providers:
- Primary: Plaid, Flinks
- Fallback: MX, Fincity, Inverite
- Manual document verification for additional income (e.g., child support)

[View Bank Connect Step Details](/steps/bank-connect)

### 5. Residence History
Validates three years of residence history.
- Previous addresses
- Manual verification through documentation
- Advisor review process

[View Residence History Step Details](/steps/residence-history)

### 6. Assets Verification
Evaluates financial assets and property ownership.
- Total asset value calculation
- Property ownership verification
- Mortgage and HELOC verification
- Net worth determination

[View Assets Step Details](/steps/assets)

### 7. Credit Report
Pulls comprehensive credit information from Equifax and TransUnion.
- Credit score
- Payment history
- Active liabilities
- Bankruptcy records
- Consumer proposals

[View Credit Report Step Details](/steps/credit-report)

### 8. Background Check
Performs comprehensive background screening via Persona.
- Watchlist verification
- Sanctions check

## Success Criteria

A successful tenant screening requires:
1. Verified identity matching government-issued documents
2. Confirmed employment history with stable income
3. Verified residence history
4. Satisfactory credit score and payment history
5. Clear background check results
6. Sufficient assets for required payments

## Error Handling

- If any required step fails, the flow will pause and request manual review
- Multiple provider options ensure redundancy for critical verifications
- Manual document upload fallback available for all automated steps
