---
title: "Credit Report"
description: "The credit report step retrieves and analyzes an individual's credit history from major credit bureaus."
---

## Configuration

```json
{
    "id": "6797e02da4c4910bc0f1d493",
    "name": "CREDIT_REPORT",
    "control_flow": {
        "condition": {
            "condition_operator": ">=",
            "condition_field": "credit_score",
            "condition_value": 700
        },
        "if_goto": "6797fe5e683fb1e8abe7d853",
        "else_goto": "6797e02da4c4910bc0f1d493"
    },
    "provider": ["Equifax", "TransUnion"]
}
```

## Config Fields

### Required Fields
- `id`: Unique identifier for the verification step
- `name`: Step type identifier (CREDIT_REPORT)

### Control Flow
Determines the next step based on credit score:
- If `credit_score` is greater than or equal to 700, proceeds to step `6797fe5e683fb1e8abe7d853`
- Otherwise, goes to step `6797e02da4c4910bc0f1d493`

### Providers
- Primary: Equifax for credit report and score
- Fallback: TransUnion as secondary provider if Equifax fails

## Output Fields

### Status Information
- `status`: Current status of the verification (e.g., "COMPLETED")
- `credit_score`: Current credit score (300-850)

### Credit Summary
- `summary`: Overview of credit accounts and balances
  - `accounts`: Count of total, open, closed, and delinquent accounts
  - `balances`: Total balance and monthly payment obligations
  - `utilization`: Credit utilization statistics

### Account Information
- `accounts`: List of credit accounts
  - `type`: Type of credit account
  - `institution`: Financial institution details
  - `status`: Current account status
  - `balance`: Credit limit and current balance
  - `payment_history`: Payment history including late payments

### Credit Inquiries
- `inquiries`: Information about credit inquiries
  - `hard_inquiries`: Hard inquiries in the last 24 months

### Public Records
- `public_records`: Public record items
  - Count of bankruptcies, tax liens, and civil judgments

### Alerts
- `alerts`: Active alerts on credit file
  - Presence of fraud alerts or security freezes

### Verification Details
- `verification`: Information about the verification process
  - `provider`: Which provider performed the verification
  - `verified_at`: When verification was completed
